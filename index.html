What you should know about

AREWA TECH FEST
2024

Arewa Tech Fest is a pioneering technology festival designed to ignite innovation, inspire tech enthusiasts, and build a thriving ecosystem for technology-driven solutions across Northern Nigeria. With its inaugural edition taking place in Kano, this multi-city festival aims to foster collaboration, showcase cutting-edge technologies, and support the growth of tech startups in the region.
Our mission is to create a platform that connects innovators, entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders, fostering a vibrant tech community in Northern Nigeria. We aim to empower the next generation of tech talent, drive economic growth, and bridge the gap between tradition and modern technology.

Arewa Tech Fest 2024 is set to disrupt the seemingly low level of technological advancement in Northern Nigeria.
